F5-TTS REST API

Base URL: /api (port 80) or http://HOST:8301 (direct)
All endpoints are unauthenticated on direct port access.

GET/voice/tts Generate speech → WAV

Returns audio/wav — 16-bit PCM mono, 24 000 Hz.

textstringRequired. Text to synthesize.
speedfloatSpeech rate. Default 1.0. Range 0.5–2.0.
nfe_stepint 4–64Diffusion steps. Default 32. Higher = better quality, slower.
cfg_strengthfloat 0–5Voice fidelity. Default 2.0. Recommended 1.5–2.5.
cross_fade_durationfloat 0–1Segment smoothing. Default 0.15.
sway_sampling_coeffloatExperimental sampler. Default -1 (off).
seedintReproducibility seed. Default -1 (random).

curl:

curl "http://HOST:8301/voice/tts?text=Hello+world" -o out.wav

Python:

resp = requests.get("http://HOST:8301/voice/tts", params={"text":"Hello"})
open("out.wav","wb").write(resp.content)

Training Guide — step by step

Step 1 — Record your voice

Record 5–20 minutes of clean speech. Read naturally, with clear pauses at commas and periods. Use a quiet room. Save as WAV (mono, 22050 or 44100 Hz).

Step 2 — Upload to Fine-tune UI

Open Fine-tune. Go to Prepare tab. Upload your audio file. The system will auto-transcribe with Whisper and split into segments.

Step 3 — Configure training

Go to Train tab. Set:
• Dataset name — your project name
• Epochs — 100–130 for a good result
• Batch size — 800 (GPU), 200 (CPU)
• Leave other settings at default

Step 4 — Monitor training

Open TensorBoard to watch the loss curve. Training is complete when loss stabilizes (typically below 0.5).

Step 5 — Test checkpoints

Open Inference. Load your checkpoint from /data/checkpoints/your_name/. Test different checkpoints (the best is often 70–90% through training).

Step 6 — Deploy

Edit .env and set MODEL_PATH to your best checkpoint, REF_AUDIO to a clean 15-second sample, REF_TEXT to its transcript. Then run ./start.sh.

Tips for better pauses at commas

When reading your training script, pause clearly at every comma. In generated text, use — (em-dash) or .... (four dots with space) for longer pauses than a regular comma produces.
